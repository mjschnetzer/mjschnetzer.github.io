Vlad's Homeland

Citizen of World's Greatest Nation

My Hometown - Rybinsk, Russia

As a boy I spent most of my time working and playing at my parent's
farm. It was outside of Rybinsk, a small city located at the mouth of
the Volga river with beautiful architecture. As I started going to school
I spent more time in the city, finding odd jobs and causing trouble. I
still travel to the area to go to the theater with my mother or drink
with old friends.





Current Residence - Moscow, Russia

I learned I enjoyed life in the city. After completing secondary school
in Rybinsk I decided to move to a cheap apartment in the Barrikadnaya
Area. I worked as a ganitor for an industrial complex before finding an
opening as a cab driver. I worked my way up to move into a flat in
Tverskaya. My building is near many bars I frequent and my favorite
resturaunt, Khachapuri. After work I relax and view at the city from my
11th floor balcony.
